I am nothing if not what I do in the world.

My personal life is fairly quiet. My weekdays are usually work and whatever chores are necessary for my continued being, with some evenings being occupied by reading or going to the gym. On the weekends I try to be active by hiking, working on projects, or going out to try new things.

Some things that I enjoy are watching the remarkably human cartoon television show Futurama, playing Dungeon and Dragons with my friends, and singing in the car (I am working towards my breakthrough debut in my apartment). On an average day, you might find me listening to music and working away at whatever I have put before myself.

I have read a decent amount of philosophy and love to talk about it. Feel free to start a conversation with me about anything; my curiosity for the many different ways in which we humans exist in the world is without limit and I am always interested in learning more of the human experience.
