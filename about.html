My personal life is fairly quiet. My weekdays are usually work and whatever chores are necessary for my continued being, with some evenings being occupied by reading or going to the gym. On the weekends I try to be active by hiking, working on projects, or occasionally going out to try new things.

Some things that I enjoy are watching movies that comment on the human experience (I like Guillermo Del Toro), playing Dungeon and Dragons with my friends, and singing whenever I can find the courage to disturb my neighbors. On an average day, you might find me listening to music or white noise and working away at whatever I have put before myself.

I have read a decent amount of philosophy and enjoy talking about it. I think there is a lot to be said about the emerging profession that is the writing of software, and am very interested in learning more about where the industry has been and where it is going. Feel free to start a conversation with me about anything; I am a very curious person.
