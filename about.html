My personal life is fairly quiet. My weekdays are usually work and chores, with some evenings being occupied by reading or going to the gym. On the weekends I try to be active by hiking, working on projects, or occasionally going out to try new things. The latest book I both started and finished was The Most Human Human.

Some things that I enjoy are watching movies that comment on the human experience (I like Guillermo Del Toro), being out in the wild on backpacking trips, and singing whenever I can find the courage to disturb my neighbors. On an average day, you might find me listening to music or white noise and working away at whatever I have put before myself.

I have read a decent amount of philosophy and enjoy talking about it. Feel free to start a conversation with me about anything; I am a very curious person.
