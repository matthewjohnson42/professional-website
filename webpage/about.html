My personal life is fairly quiet. My weekdays are usually work and chores, with evenings occupied by personal work and studies. On the weekends I try to be active by hiking, working on projects, or occasionally going out to try something new.

I enjoy reading. The latest book I both started and finished was The Most Human Human. There are plenty more by William James, Steven Pinker, Marcus Aurelius, and others that sit either half finished or un-started.

I also enjoy watching movies that comment on the human experience (I am a fan of Guillermo Del Toro), being out in the wild on backpacking trips, and singing whenever I can find the courage to disturb my neighbors.

I am a fairly open person. Feel free to reach out to start a conversation with me.
