Hello! Welcome to my website.

I have created this website for the convenience of yourself, the individual who has chosen to take the time to investigate the contents of my eponymous website. My hope is that any questions you might have about myself are given greater insight in the course of your visit to this site.

I graduated cum laude in December 2017 from Augsburg University with a Bachelor of the Sciences degree in Computer Science and a Bachelor of the Arts degree in Computational Philosophy. I chose to study these subjects as I have a long standing interest in the logical structures that give form to the world of the individual and that are revealed to us through a precise inspection of the intersubjective world of the social organism. Computer Science was a practical application of this curiosity and Computational Philosophy, if nothing more, was a wonderful excuse to be able to study the foundations of the expression of this curiosity as present in the traditions of western philosophy. I also took a minor in Mathematics while studying at Augsburg University.

Prior to my studies at Augsburg University in Minneapolis, Minnesota, I had worked at United HealthGroup where I performed the duties of a software quality assurance consultant. These duties involved developing an understanding of the function of the project I was assigned to and using that understanding to ensure that such a function was realized with minimal errors. Before that, I had also spent time studying at Century College in White Bear Lake, Minnesota. It was there that I determined that the study of Computer Science would be a profitable course of action that I would be comfortable with pursuing.
